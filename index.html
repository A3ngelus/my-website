What is a paladin?

A paladin is a holy warrior who combines martial prowess with divine magic to fight evil and protect the innocent.

What is a death knight?

In short a death knight is a unholy knight that was brought back from the dead. In some cases such as WoW they are the otherside of the coin using necromatic and shadow powers instead of holy powers.
