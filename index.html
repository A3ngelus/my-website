Death Knights

In most media, the death knight is the anti-paladin. Functioning as a paladin but has been risen from the dead and instead of usinig holy/divine magic they mostly use necromancy or shadow magic.

What is a paladin?

A paladin is a holy warrior who combines martial prowess with divine magic to fight evil and protect the innocent.

Paladin history

Paladins originated in medieval European legends as the twelve heroic knights of Charlemagne’s court, known for their bravery, chivalry, and loyalty. Over time, the concept evolved, especially in fantasy literature and games, where paladins became holy warriors imbued with divine power, often serving as champions of justice and protectors against evil.
